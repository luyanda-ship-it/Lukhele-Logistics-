Our Services

Comprehensive coal transportation and mining logistics solutions.

What We Offer

End-to-end logistics solutions tailored to the mining industry's unique demands.

Coal Transportation

Our core service — reliable, efficient coal haulage from mine to destination. We transport coal to power stations, processing plants, rail depots, and export terminals across South Africa. Our fleet is maintained to the highest standards to ensure on-time delivery every time.

Contract Haulage

Flexible and scalable contract haulage solutions tailored to your production schedule. Whether you need short-term surge capacity or long-term dedicated haulage, we provide reliable service with transparent pricing and clear performance metrics.

Fleet Management

Professional fleet management services covering vehicle procurement, maintenance scheduling, route optimization, fuel management, and driver management. We help mining operations maximise fleet utilisation while minimising downtime and operating costs.

Supply Chain Coordination

End-to-end supply chain coordination that streamlines the movement of coal from mine to end-user. We manage the logistics chain — scheduling, routing, loading coordination, documentation, and delivery confirmation — so you can focus on production.

Mining Logistics

Specialised logistics support grounded in hands-on underground mining experience. As an Underground Shift Boss with Blasting Certification, our founder brings a deep understanding of mine-site logistics, safety protocols, and operational coordination that general logistics providers simply cannot match.

Coal Delivery Solutions

Customised coal delivery programs designed to meet specific volume, schedule, and quality requirements. We work closely with clients to develop delivery solutions that align with production targets and end-user specifications.

Bulk Material Haulage

Beyond coal, we transport a wide range of bulk materials including aggregates, mining supplies, and industrial materials. Our fleet is equipped to handle various load types across different terrains and site conditions.

Mining Support Services

Comprehensive operational support including logistics coordination, site logistics management, equipment movement, and supply delivery. We help mining operations run smoothly by taking care of the logistics so they can focus on extraction and production.
