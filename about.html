Luyanda Lukhele — Founder & Operations Lead

Meet Our Founder

Luyanda Lukhele brings years of hands-on mining operations experience to every logistics contract. As an Underground Shift Boss with Underground Blasting Certification and a Valid Blasting License, Luyanda has worked directly in the heart of South Africa's coal mining industry.

This background gives Lukhele Logistics a unique advantage — we don't just transport coal, we understand the entire mining operation. We know the production pressures, the safety imperatives, and the supply chain challenges that mines face every day.

Lukhele Logistics was founded to bridge the gap between mining operations and logistics. Our founder's direct experience as a Shift Boss means we speak the language of mining, understand the urgency of production targets, and are uncompromising on safety.
